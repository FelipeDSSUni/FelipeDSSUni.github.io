High Stakes







High Stakes is a co-op puzzle platformer where you play as two vampires attempting to clear their castle of vampire hunters.

I worked as the sole programmer on this game within a team of three people. One of the main mechanics of the game is the ability to swap between a platforming vampire form (that can interact with buttons and levers) and a floating mist form (that can travel through obstacles).
